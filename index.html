About Us

Enviro Building Company is dedicated to creating sustainable and eco-friendly building solutions. Learn more about our mission and values.

Our Founder

With over 10 years of experience in the construction industry, Marlena Skibicka stands as a leader in sustainable and cost-effective construction solutions. Serving as the Managing Director at Construction, Marlena steers the strategic direction, financial performance, and operational excellence of the company. She has successfully overseen multiple residential projects, ranging from small renovations to new builds, consistently delivering high-quality results within budget and time constraints. Marlena boasts a strong background in spatial planning, environment and sustainability, and pre-contract cost management, complemented by certifications in SMSTS and RICS. She is passionate about creating innovative and environmentally friendly designs that meet the needs and expectations of clients and stakeholders. Additionally, Marlena is committed to fostering a collaborative and inclusive culture within the team, ensuring that everyone can contribute their skills and ideas to achieve their shared vision.

Our Vision

Enviro Building Company envisions a transformative future where the realms of construction and environmental stewardship merge seamlessly, leading the forefront of sustainable building practices. Grounded in a robust foundation of innovation, quality, and unwavering commitment to sustainability, the company's overarching mission encompasses delivering cost-effective construction solutions that markedly reduce environmental impact. With a steadfast dedication to incorporating advanced green technologies and sustainable materials in each of its projects, Enviro Building Company ambitiously aims to redefine industry standards in energy efficiency and eco-friendly architectural design. By unequivocally prioritizing the health and well-being of our planet and its diverse inhabitants, the company ardently hopes to catalyze a significant paradigm shift towards more conscientious and responsible building practices throughout the construction industry. Emboldened by a deep-seated commitment to excellence and a fervent passion for sustainability, Enviro Building Company is unwavering in its pursuit to not only meet but exceed the evolving needs and expectations of its esteemed clients. In doing so, it endeavors to forge a lasting, positive impact on the global environment, heralding a new era of eco-conscious development.
